My Bio

Clint was born and raised in the Boise, Idaho valley, went to school at BYU-Idaho and got his bachelor's degree in Music Education. He served a mission for his church in Argentina for 2 years and gained a lot of experience helping those in need. Now you can find him working in Florida at Walt Disney World! He's worked at Disney since 2013 in Attractions, Merchandise, and now as a performer portraying various iconic character throughout the parks. He's currently working on a second bachelor's degree in Software Development.
